MAA AMC 10 Mock Site

The purpose of this site is to provide a resource for training for the AMC 10 by solving problems on a realistic testing interface similar to the contest.

In the spirit of being as realistic as possible to the real contest, you cannot pause or exit the test and come back later; the problem set you chose must be completed in block.

About the AMC 10

The AMC 10, administered by the Mathematical Association of America (MAA) is a 25-question, 75-minute multiple-choice exam focused on utilizing high school mathematics up to 10th grade level to solve problems centered around demonstrating strong problem-solving and critial thinking skills.

The AMC 10 is the gateway exam to farther mathematical opportunities, the most immediate being the American Invitational Mathematics Examination (AIME). Around the top 6% scorers of the AMC 10 are invited to take the AIME. Good scoring in both the AMC 10 and AIME leads to the qualification for the prestigious United States of America Junior Mathematics Olympiad (USAJMO), which opens one's path to many more mathematical opportunities in competition.
